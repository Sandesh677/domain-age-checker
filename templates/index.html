What is Domain Age?

Domain age refers to how long a domain name has been registered and active on the internet. Older domains are often considered more trustworthy by search engines like Google.

Why Domain Age Matters?

  • ✔ Helps evaluate website trust and authority
  • ✔ Important for SEO ranking signals
  • ✔ Useful for buying expired or old domains

How This Tool Works

This tool fetches WHOIS data of a domain and calculates the age based on its creation date. You get accurate results in seconds.
